<p><p>Semiconducting Substrates refer to materials that have a high electrical conductivity, commonly used in electronic devices. These substrates are essential for the fabrication of integrated circuits and other electronic components. On the other hand, Semi-Insulating Substrates have low electrical conductivity and are used in applications where high resistance and low loss characteristics are required. Both types of substrates play a crucial role in the semiconductor industry, catering to different needs and applications.</p></p>
