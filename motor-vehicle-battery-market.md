<p><ul><li>Maintenance-free Battery</li><li>Conventional Battery</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The motor vehicle battery market can be divided into two main categories: maintenance-free batteries and conventional batteries. Maintenance-free batteries, as the name suggests, do not require regular maintenance such as adding water or checking electrolyte levels. These batteries are sealed and designed to last longer without any upkeep. On the other hand, conventional batteries need periodic maintenance to ensure they function properly and have a shorter lifespan compared to maintenance-free batteries. Both types have their own advantages and are chosen based on individual preferences and needs.</p></p>
