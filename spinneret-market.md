<p><ul><li>Melt Spinning Spinneret</li><li>Wet Spinneret</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Spinneret market is segmented into different types based on the spinning process. Melt spinning spinnerets are used in producing fibers by melting a polymer and extruding it through the spinneret holes. This type of spinneret is widely used in synthetic fiber production. On the other hand, wet spinnerets are utilized in spinning fibers from a solution of polymer in a solvent. Each type of spinneret caters to specific applications and industries, offering a diverse range of solutions for fiber production.</p></p>
