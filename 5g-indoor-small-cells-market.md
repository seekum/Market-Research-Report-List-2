<p><strong>The 5G Indoor Small Cells Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Sub-6GHz</li><li>mmWAVE</li></ul></p>
<p><p>5G Indoor Small Cells are divided into two main market types based on the frequency bands they operate with: Sub-6GHz and mmWave. Sub-6GHz small cells utilize frequencies below 6GHz, providing wider coverage and better penetration through walls, making them suitable for indoor deployments in buildings and offices. On the other hand, mmWave small cells operate in higher frequency bands, above 24GHz, offering faster data speeds but shorter range, making them ideal for high-capacity indoor environments like stadiums, shopping malls, and convention centers.</p></p>
