<p><strong>The LED Chip and Module Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Lateral Chip</li><li>Vertical Chip</li><li>Flip Chip</li></ul></p>
<p><p>LED chip market can be categorized into lateral chip, vertical chip, and flip chip types. Lateral chip technology involves the movement of electrons in a horizontal direction, resulting in higher brightness. Vertical chip technology allows for better heat dissipation and improved light extraction efficiency. Flip chip technology enables direct connection of the LED chip to the substrate, enhancing thermal management and enabling higher power output. Each type has its advantages and is utilized in various applications based on specific requirements.</p></p>
