<p><ul><li>Cased Hole Wireline Logging Services</li><li>Open Hole Wireline Logging Services</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Wireline Logging Services are divided into two main markets - Cased Hole Wireline Logging Services and Open Hole Wireline Logging Services. Cased Hole Logging involves running tools through the casing of a well to evaluate the condition of the casing and formation behind it. Open Hole Logging involves evaluating the formation before casing is installed. Both types of services play a crucial role in providing essential data for well evaluation, reservoir characterization, and production optimization in the oil and gas industry.</p></p>
