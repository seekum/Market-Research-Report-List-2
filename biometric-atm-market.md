<p><p>A Biometric ATM is an automated teller machine (ATM) that uses biometric authentication technology, such as fingerprint scanning, iris recognition, or facial recognition, to verify the identity of users accessing the machine. Overall, the Biometric ATM Market is poised for significant growth and innovation in the coming years.</p></p>
