<p><p>Track log loaders are specialized equipment used in forestry operations that have rubber tracks similar to those on a bulldozer or excavator. They are designed to traverse rough terrain with minimal impact on the environment. Wheel log loaders, on the other hand, have conventional tires and are suitable for more stable and even surfaces. Both types of log loaders are used for loading, unloading, and transporting logs in the forestry industry, but their track systems determine their suitability for different terrains.</p></p>
