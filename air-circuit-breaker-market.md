<p><strong>The Air Circuit Breaker Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Air Blast Circuit Breaker</li><li>Plain Air Circuit Breaker</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Air Circuit Breakers are classified into two main types - Air Blast Circuit Breaker and Plain Air Circuit Breaker. </p><p>Air Blast Circuit Breakers use compressed air to extinguish the arc formed during the interruption of current, making them more efficient and suitable for high voltage applications. </p><p>On the other hand, Plain Air Circuit Breakers rely solely on atmospheric air for arc extinction, making them simpler in design and more cost-effective. Both types play a crucial role in electrical protection systems in various industries.</p></p>
