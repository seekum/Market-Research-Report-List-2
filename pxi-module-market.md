<p><p>PXI Module Market Types are categorized into PXI RF Multiplexers, PXI Switches, PXI Signal Generators, PXI Signal Analyzers, and Others. PXI RF Multiplexers are used for routing RF signals to different inputs/outputs. PXI Switches allow for switching between different signals or connections. PXI Signal Generators are used to generate signals for testing purposes. PXI Signal Analyzers are used to analyze and measure signals. The Others category includes various modules that do not fit into the above categories, such as power supplies or digital I/O modules.</p></p>
