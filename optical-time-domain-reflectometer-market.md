<p><ul><li>Singlemode OTDR</li><li>Multimode OTDR</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Optical Time-Domain Reflectometer (OTDR) market is segmented into two main types: Singlemode OTDR and Multimode OTDR. Singlemode OTDRs are designed for use with singlemode optical fibers, which are used for long-distance communication. They provide high resolution and accuracy for detecting issues in long fiber networks. </p><p>On the other hand, Multimode OTDRs are used with multimode fibers, which are typically used for shorter distances. They are more cost-effective and are suitable for troubleshooting in local area networks or data centers.</p></p>
