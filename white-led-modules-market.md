<p><p>White LED modules can be categorized into three main types based on their power output. Low power modules typically have a power rating of 0.3 watts or below, making them suitable for decorative and accent lighting applications. Middle power modules fall within the range of 0.3 to 0.5 watts, offering a balance between brightness and energy efficiency. High power modules, on the other hand, have a power rating of 1 watt and above, making them ideal for applications requiring high levels of brightness and intensity.</p></p>
