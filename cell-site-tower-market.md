<p><p>Cell site towers are commonly classified into three main market types: ground-based towers, rooftop towers, and others. Ground-based towers are structures built from the ground up specifically for housing cell site equipment. Rooftop towers are installed on existing buildings to minimize the need for new structures. The "others" category may include alternative tower types such as monopoles or stealth towers. Each market type offers unique advantages and challenges for cell site operators looking to expand their coverage and capacity.</p></p>
