<p><p>Fluid rotary unions are rotary connectors that allow the transfer of fluids between stationary and rotating components. The market for fluid rotary unions can be categorized into three types based on the number of channels they have: single channel, double channel, and multi-channel. Single channel rotary unions accommodate the transfer of one fluid stream, double channel rotary unions allow for two separate fluid streams, and multi-channel rotary unions are designed to transfer multiple fluid streams simultaneously. Each type caters to specific industrial applications requiring different levels of fluid transfer.</p></p>
