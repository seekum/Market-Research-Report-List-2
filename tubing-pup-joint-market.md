<p><p>Tubing pup joints are available in two main market types: hot rolled and cold rolled. Hot rolled pup joints are manufactured at high temperatures, allowing for faster production and a rougher surface finish. Cold rolled pup joints are manufactured at lower temperatures, resulting in a smoother surface finish and improved dimensional accuracy. Both types serve the same purpose of connecting sections of tubing in oil and gas wells, but the choice between hot rolled and cold rolled depends on specific project requirements and preferences.</p></p>
