<p><strong>The CAM Software Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>2-D</li><li>3-D</li></ul></p>
<p><p>CAM software is typically classified into two main market types: 2-D and 3-D. 2-D CAM software is designed for simple, flat designs and is commonly used for industries such as woodworking and sheet metal fabrication. On the other hand, 3-D CAM software is more advanced and is used for complex, three-dimensional designs commonly found in industries like aerospace, automotive, and medical devices. Both types of CAM software offer tools for creating toolpaths, simulating machining processes, and generating G-code for CNC machines.</p></p>
