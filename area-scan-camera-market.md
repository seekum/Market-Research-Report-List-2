<p><p>Area scan cameras are commonly classified into two main types: CMOS and CCD. CMOS cameras are known for their fast readout speeds and low power consumption, making them ideal for applications requiring high frame rates. On the other hand, CCD cameras are known for their high image quality and sensitivity, making them suitable for low light imaging. Both CMOS and CCD cameras have their strengths and weaknesses, and the choice between the two depends on the specific requirements of the application.</p></p>
