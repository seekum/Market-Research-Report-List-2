<p><p>Bionic exoskeletons market consists of two main types - active exoskeletons and passive exoskeletons. Active exoskeletons are powered and designed to augment the wearer's movements, providing assistance and enhancing strength and endurance. On the other hand, passive exoskeletons do not require any external power source and mainly work by redistributing weight and reducing strain on the body. Both types of exoskeletons cater to different needs and applications, offering solutions for rehabilitation, healthcare, military, and industrial sectors.</p></p>
