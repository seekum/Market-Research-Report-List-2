<p><p>The Hermetic Doors Market includes two main types: Hermetic Sliding Doors and Hermetic Hinged Doors. Hermetic Sliding Doors are designed to slide open horizontally, creating a seamless and airtight seal when closed. These doors are commonly used in hospitals, laboratories, and clean rooms to maintain a sterile environment. Hermetic Hinged Doors, on the other hand, swing open on hinges and provide the same airtight seal as sliding doors. Both types are crucial for environments where air quality and cleanliness are paramount.</p></p>
