<p><ul><li>Active</li><li>Passive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Audio crossovers can be categorized into two main market types: Active and Passive. Active crossovers require external power to operate and can provide more precise control over frequency bands. They are commonly used in professional audio systems. Passive crossovers do not require external power and are more commonly found in home audio systems due to their simplicity and cost-effectiveness. Both types serve the same purpose of splitting audio signals into different frequency bands to ensure optimal sound quality and performance.</p></p>
