<p><p>Cable duct markets can be divided into two main types: Flexible Cable Duct and Rigid Cable Duct. Flexible cable ducts are made of materials that can bend and flex easily, making them suitable for environments where cables may need to be routed around obstacles. Rigid cable ducts, on the other hand, are made of sturdy materials that provide protection and support for cables in more stable environments. Both types of cable ducts play a crucial role in organizing and protecting cables in various settings.</p></p>
