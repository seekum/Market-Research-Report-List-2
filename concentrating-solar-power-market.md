<p><strong>The Concentrating Solar Power Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Parabolic Trough</li><li>Solar Tower</li><li>Linear Fresnel</li><li>Dish/Engine Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Concentrating Solar Power (CSP) market includes four main types of systems: Parabolic Trough, Solar Tower, Linear Fresnel, and Dish/Engine Systems. Parabolic Trough systems use curved mirrors to concentrate sunlight onto a tube filled with fluid, generating steam to drive a turbine. Solar Tower systems use a central receiver to collect sunlight reflected by a field of mirrors. Linear Fresnel systems utilize flat mirrors to concentrate sunlight onto a receiver tube. Dish/Engine Systems use a dish-shaped reflector to concentrate sunlight onto a receiver at the focal point.</p></p>
