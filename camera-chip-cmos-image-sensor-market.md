<p><strong>The Camera Chip CMOS Image Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Passive Pixel Passive Sensor (PPS)</li><li>Active Pixel Active Sensor (APS)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Camera Chip CMOS Image Sensor market includes two main types: Passive Pixel Passive Sensor (PPS) and Active Pixel Active Sensor (APS). PPS sensors convert light into electrical signals passively, resulting in lower power consumption and simpler manufacturing processes. On the other hand, APS sensors integrate amplifiers and other signal processing circuitry on each pixel, allowing for faster readout speeds and higher image quality. Both types have their own advantages and are utilized in various applications within the imaging industry.</p></p>
