<p><p>CCTV Cameras market can be divided into various types based on their design and functionality. Dome cameras are ceiling-mounted, providing a 360-degree view. Bullet cameras are long and cylindrical, ideal for outdoor use. Box cameras offer customizable lens options for specific surveillance needs. PTZ (Pan-Tilt-Zoom) cameras can be remotely controlled to pan, tilt, and zoom in on specific areas. Other types may include thermal cameras, covert cameras, and fisheye cameras, each serving unique purposes in different surveillance settings.</p></p>
