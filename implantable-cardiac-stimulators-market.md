<p><p>Implantable Cardiac Stimulators include different types such as Implantable Cardioverter Defibrillator (ICD), Pacemaker, Cardiac Resynchronization Therapy Defibrillator (CRT-D), and Cardiac Resynchronization Therapy Pacemaker (CRT-P). ICDs are used to treat abnormal heart rhythms, pacemakers help regulate the heartbeat, while CRT-Ds and CRT-Ps are used for patients with heart failure to improve heart function and reduce symptoms. Each of these devices serves a specific purpose in managing cardiac conditions and improving patient outcomes.</p></p>
