<p><p>Tool room grinding wheels are used for precision grinding and shaping of tools and machinery parts. The four main types of tool room grinding wheels are straight wheel, tapered wheel, straight cup, and dish cup. Straight wheels are used for surface grinding, while tapered wheels are used for gear grinding and tool sharpening. Straight cup wheels are ideal for cutting off and slotting operations, and dish cup wheels are used for face grinding and finishing operations. Each type of wheel offers specific advantages for different grinding applications.</p></p>
