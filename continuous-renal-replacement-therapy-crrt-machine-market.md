<p><p>Continuous Renal Replacement Therapy (CRRT) machines are used for patients with acute kidney injury. There are two main types of CRRT machines: mobile and fixed. Mobile machines are compact and can be easily transported from one patient's bedside to another, making them ideal for ICU settings. Fixed machines are larger and remain stationary in one location, commonly used in dialysis units or dedicated CRRT areas. Both types of machines serve the same purpose of providing continuous renal replacement therapy for critically ill patients.</p></p>
