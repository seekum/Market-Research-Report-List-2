<p><strong>The Communications Interface Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>1-Port</li><li>2-Port</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Communications Interface Market Types, such as 1-Port and 2-Port, refer to the number of physical connections available on the interface device for transmitting data. A 1-Port interface has a single connection point, allowing for the transfer of one data stream at a time. Conversely, a 2-Port interface has two connection points, enabling simultaneous data transfer or the option to connect two separate devices. Both types offer different levels of connectivity and flexibility based on the user's needs and requirements.</p></p>
