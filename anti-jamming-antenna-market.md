<p><p>The Anti-Jamming Antenna market includes three main types: Nulling System, Beam Steering Systems, and Civilian Systems. Nulling System antennas use multiple antennas to cancel out interfering signals. Beam Steering Systems electronically adjust the direction of the antenna to find the strongest signal. Civilian Systems are designed for commercial use and provide reliable connectivity without interference. Each type of antenna serves a specific purpose in mitigating jamming and ensuring clear communication in various applications.</p></p>
