<p><p>Video intercom devices market can be categorized into two types: Analog Type and IP Type. Analog type devices transmit audio and video signals through traditional analog wires, while IP type devices use internet protocol networks to transmit data. Analog type devices are simpler and more cost-effective, but have limited functionalities compared to IP type devices which offer advanced features such as remote access, integration with smart home systems, and cloud storage capabilities. IP type devices are gaining popularity due to their flexibility and scalability.</p></p>
