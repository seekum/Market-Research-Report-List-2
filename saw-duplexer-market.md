<p><ul><li>Unbalanced Type</li><li>Balanced Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>SAW Duplexer Market Types refer to the different designs of surface acoustic wave (SAW) duplexers used in electronic devices. Unbalanced Type duplexers have an asymmetrical design with different impedance levels on input and output ports, while Balanced Type duplexers have a symmetrical design with equal impedance levels. Unbalanced Type duplexers are commonly used in low-cost applications, while Balanced Type duplexers are preferred for high-performance systems due to their better isolation and rejection of unwanted signals. Both types cater to different market needs based on performance and cost considerations.</p></p>
