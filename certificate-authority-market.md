<p><strong>The Certificate Authority Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Extended Validation</li><li>Organization Validation</li><li>Domain Validation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Certificate Authority (CA) market is segmented into three types based on the level of validation provided: Extended Validation (EV), Organization Validation (OV), and Domain Validation (DV). EV certificates offer the highest level of validation, verifying the legal identity and physical existence of the organization. OV certificates verify the organization's identity and contact information. DV certificates only confirm ownership of the domain. Organizations can choose the level of validation based on their security needs and budget constraints.</p></p>
