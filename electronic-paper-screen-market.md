<p><p>Electronic paper screens can be classified into two main types: rigid and flexible. Rigid electronic paper screens have a solid and inflexible surface, similar to traditional paper, making them ideal for applications where a flat display is required. On the other hand, flexible electronic paper screens have a bendable or rollable surface, providing more versatility and allowing for curved or irregularly shaped displays. Both types of screens are gaining popularity in the market for their low power consumption, ease of readability, and environmental friendliness.</p></p>
