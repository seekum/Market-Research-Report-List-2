<p><strong>The Moored Buoy Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Point Mooring (SPM)</li><li>Multiple Buoy Mooring (MBM)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>A moored buoy is a type of floating structure used to anchor ships in place. Single Point Mooring (SPM) involves one buoy connecting a ship to a seabed anchor, allowing for easy loading and unloading of cargo. Multiple Buoy Mooring (MBM) utilizes several buoys arranged in a pattern to secure a ship in place, offering more stability and flexibility in handling multiple vessels. Both types of moored buoys play a crucial role in maritime operations and offshore industries.</p></p>
