<p><strong>The Water Sampler Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Automatic Water Sampler</li><li>Manual Water Sampler</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automatic water samplers are devices that collect water samples at pre-determined intervals without the need for human intervention. These samplers are commonly used in industries and environmental monitoring applications where continuous and unattended sampling is required. On the other hand, manual water samplers are operated by personnel who physically collect water samples at specific locations and times. Manual samplers are often used in research studies or in situations where on-site analysis is needed. Both types of water samplers play a crucial role in ensuring accurate and reliable water quality data.</p></p>
