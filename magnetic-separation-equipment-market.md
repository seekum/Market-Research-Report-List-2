<p><p>Magnetic separation equipment can be classified into two main types: electromagnetic and permanent. Electromagnetic separators use an electric current to generate a magnetic field, which attracts and separates magnetic materials. On the other hand, permanent magnetic separators have a permanent magnet that creates a strong magnetic field for separating materials. Both types of equipment are widely used in industries such as mining, recycling, and food processing for sorting and separating magnetic materials from non-magnetic substances.</p></p>
