<p><p>Automated injection molding machines are categorized based on their clamping force, with three main types: below 250T, 250-650T, and above 650T. Machines with clamping force below 250T are suitable for small to medium-sized production runs. Those with clamping force of 250-650T are ideal for medium-sized production runs. Machines with clamping force above 650T are designed for large-scale production runs. Each type of machine offers different capacities and efficiency levels to cater to varying production needs in the manufacturing industry.</p></p>
