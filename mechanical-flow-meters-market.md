<p><strong>The Mechanical Flow Meters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Vortex Shedding Flowmeter</li><li>Vane/Piston Flowmeter</li><li>Paddlewheel Flowmeter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Mechanical flow meters are used to measure the flow rate of fluids in various industries. The Vortex Shedding Flowmeter works by creating vortices in the flow stream, which can then be counted to determine the flow rate. Vane/Piston Flowmeters use a moving vane or piston to measure flow velocity. Paddlewheel Flowmeters have a rotating paddlewheel that is turned by the flow of the fluid. Each type of flow meter has its own advantages and applications depending on the specific requirements of the industry.</p></p>
