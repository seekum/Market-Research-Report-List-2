<p><strong>The Distribution Transformers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-Phase</li><li>Three-Phase</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Distribution transformers are classified into two main types: Single-Phase and Three-Phase. Single-Phase distribution transformers are used in residential areas and small commercial buildings where the electricity demand is relatively low. Three-Phase distribution transformers, on the other hand, are used in industrial settings and large commercial buildings where the electricity demand is high. Single-Phase transformers have one primary and one secondary winding, while Three-Phase transformers have three primary and three secondary windings, making them more efficient for handling higher power loads.</p></p>
