<p><strong>The Electronic Compass Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Plane Electronic Compass</li><li>Three Dimensional Electronic Compass</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electronic compasses can be classified into two main types based on their functionality: plane electronic compass and three-dimensional electronic compass. Plane electronic compasses provide heading information along a two-dimensional plane, typically used in applications where elevation is not a critical factor. Three-dimensional electronic compasses, on the other hand, provide heading information in three-dimensional space, taking into account elevation, pitch, and roll. These compasses are widely used in navigation systems for aircraft, ships, and vehicles where accurate heading information is essential for precise navigation.</p></p>
