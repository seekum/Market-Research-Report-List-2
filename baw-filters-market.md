<p><ul><li>FBAR BAW Filters</li><li>SMR BAW Filters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>BAW Filters market consists of two main types: FBAR BAW Filters and SMR BAW Filters. FBAR BAW Filters use thin-film bulk acoustic wave technology to provide high performance in wireless communication devices. On the other hand, SMR BAW Filters utilize single-mode resonators for improved filter response and efficiency. Both types play a crucial role in enhancing the performance of various electronic devices such as smartphones, tablets, and base stations by providing high-quality signal filtering and interference suppression.</p></p>
