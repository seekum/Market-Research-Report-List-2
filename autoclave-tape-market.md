<p><p>Autoclave tape is a type of adhesive tape that is used to indicate whether sterilization conditions in an autoclave have been met. It changes color when exposed to high temperatures commonly used in autoclaves, allowing users to easily verify the completion of the sterilization process. Autoclave tape is widely used in medical and laboratory settings to ensure the sterility of equipment and supplies.</p><p>The Autoclave Tape Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 5.40% during the forecast period. The market is also benefitting from technological advancements in autoclave tape, leading to the development of more accurate and efficient products.</p><p>One of the latest trends in the autoclave tape market is the increasing adoption of self-adhering autoclave tape, which eliminates the need for additional adhesives during the sterilization process. This type of autoclave tape is easy to apply and remove, making it a preferred choice for many users. Additionally, advancements in color-changing technology are leading to the development of autoclave tape that changes color more quickly and prominently, further enhancing the reliability of sterilization monitoring.</p></p>
