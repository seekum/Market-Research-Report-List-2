<p><p>Micro SD cards are categorized into three main market types based on their storage capacity: SD (8M-2G), SDHC (2G-32G), and SDXC (32G-400G). SD cards have a storage capacity ranging from 8MB to 2GB, while SDHC cards can store data from 2GB to 32GB. SDXC cards have the highest storage capacity, ranging from 32GB to 400GB. These market types cater to different consumer needs for storing various amounts of data, from small files to large multimedia content.</p></p>
