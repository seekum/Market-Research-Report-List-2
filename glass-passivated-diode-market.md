<p><p>Glass Passivated Diodes are semiconductor devices used for rectifying AC to DC current. Fast Recovery Diodes are a type of Glass Passivated Diode characterized by their high switching speeds, making them ideal for applications requiring fast switching times. Ordinary Diodes, on the other hand, have slower switching speeds but are more commonly used for general-purpose rectification. Both types of diodes play a crucial role in various electronic circuits and power supply systems, with Fast Recovery Diodes being preferred for high-speed applications and Ordinary Diodes for standard rectification tasks.</p></p>
