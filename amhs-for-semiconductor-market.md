<p><p>Automated Material Handling Systems (AMHS) play a crucial role in the semiconductor market types such as Stocker (STK), Overhead Hoist Transfer (OHT), Overhead Shuttle (OHS), Rail Guided Vehicle (RGV), and Automated Guided Vehicle (AGV). STK systems store and retrieve materials, OHT systems transfer materials using overhead hoists, OHS systems shuttle materials overhead, RGV systems transport materials on rail-guided vehicles, and AGV systems automate material transport with guided vehicles. Each system enhances efficiency and productivity in semiconductor manufacturing processes.</p></p>
