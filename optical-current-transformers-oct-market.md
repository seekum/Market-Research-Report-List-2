<p><p>The Optical Current Transformers (OCT) market consists of various types such as Magneto Optical Current Transformer (MOCT), Fiber Optical Current Transformer (FOCT), Electronic Fiber Optical Current Transformer (EFOCT), and Flexible Electronic Fiber Optical Current Transformer (FEFOCT). MOCT uses the Faraday effect to measure current, while FOCT utilizes fiber optics for accurate measurement. EFOCT incorporates electronic components for signal processing, and FEFOCT offers flexibility in installation. Each type has unique features and applications, catering to different needs in the electrical power industry.</p></p>
