<p><p>Hirsutism treatment market can be categorized into three main types - idiopathic hirsutism, secondary hirsutism, and other forms of hirsutism. Idiopathic hirsutism refers to excessive hair growth in women with no identifiable cause. Secondary hirsutism is caused by underlying medical conditions such as hormonal imbalances or polycystic ovary syndrome. Other forms of hirsutism encompass various rare causes of excessive hair growth, including medications and certain genetic conditions. Treatment options may vary depending on the specific type of hirsutism.</p></p>
