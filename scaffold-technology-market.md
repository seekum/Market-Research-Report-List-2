<p><p>Scaffold technology in the market can be segmented into four main types: Cell Attachment and Migration, Nutrients and Products Diffusion, Cell Phase Behavior Modification, and Other. </p><p>Cell Attachment and Migration scaffolds provide a surface for cells to attach and move within a 3D environment. Nutrients and Products Diffusion scaffolds allow for the transfer of essential nutrients and waste products between cells and their surroundings. Cell Phase Behavior Modification scaffolds influence cell behavior, such as proliferation or differentiation. Other scaffolds may include those designed for drug delivery, tissue regeneration, or organ-on-a-chip applications.</p></p>
