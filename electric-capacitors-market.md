<p><ul><li>Ceramic Capacitor</li><li>Film/Paper Capacitors</li><li>Aluminium Capacitors</li><li>Tantalum/Niobium Capacitors</li><li>Double-Layer/Super capacitors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electric capacitors are categorized into different types based on their construction and material. Ceramic capacitors are compact and commonly used in electronic devices. Film/paper capacitors offer high capacitance values and low leakage currents. Aluminium capacitors are used in power applications due to their high capacitance. Tantalum/niobium capacitors offer high reliability and stability. Double-layer/supercapacitors have high energy density and are used in applications requiring quick energy storage and release. Each type of capacitor serves specific purposes in various industries.</p></p>
