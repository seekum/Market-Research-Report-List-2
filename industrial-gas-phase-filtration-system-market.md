<p><p>The industrial gas phase filtration system market includes various types such as activated carbon, potassium permanganate, and blend filtration. Activated carbon filters are widely used for adsorption of organic compounds, odors, and gases. Potassium permanganate filters are effective in removing sulfur oxides, nitrogen oxides, and volatile organic compounds. Blend filtration systems combine different media such as activated carbon and potassium permanganate to provide comprehensive gas phase filtration for industrial applications. Each type offers unique benefits and applications in air purification and gas treatment processes.</p></p>
