<p><p>A polymer capacitor is a type of capacitor that uses conductive organic polymer as electrolyte instead of liquid or solid electrolyte found in traditional capacitors. This allows for higher capacitance values, lower equivalent series resistance (ESR) and lower inductance compared to other types of capacitors. Polymer capacitors are widely used in various applications such as consumer electronics, industrial equipment, automotive electronics, telecommunications equipment, and more.</p><p>The Polymer Capacitor Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 8.10% during the forecast period.
