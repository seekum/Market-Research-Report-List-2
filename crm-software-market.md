<p><p>CRM software is available in two main market types: On-Premise and Cloud Based. On-premise CRM requires installation and maintenance on the company's own servers, providing more control and customization options but at a higher cost. Cloud-based CRM, on the other hand, is hosted on the vendor's servers and accessed through the internet, offering lower upfront costs, scalability, and ease of access from anywhere. Each market type has its own benefits and limitations, catering to different business needs.</p></p>
