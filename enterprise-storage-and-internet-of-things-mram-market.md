<p><p>Enterprise storage MRAM market includes both Toggle MRAM and STT-MRAM types. Toggle MRAM utilizes electric current to switch the magnetization of the memory cells, providing fast read and write speeds. STT-MRAM, on the other hand, uses spin transfer torque to achieve non-volatile memory, making it more energy efficient. Both types of MRAM are being increasingly adopted in enterprise storage systems due to their high performance, low power consumption, and reliability. In the Internet of Things market, MRAM technology is poised to play a key role in providing fast and reliable data storage solutions for connected devices.</p></p>
