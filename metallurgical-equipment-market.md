<p><p>Metallurgical equipment market consists of various types of machinery used in different stages of metal production processes. Ironmaking equipment includes blast furnaces and direct reduction plants that convert iron ore into molten iron. Steelmaking equipment involves converters, electric arc furnaces, and ladle furnaces used to produce steel from iron. Metal rolling machines are used in the final stage of metal production to shape and compress metal into desired forms such as sheets, plates, and bars. These equipment types play a crucial role in the production of metals for various industries.</p></p>
