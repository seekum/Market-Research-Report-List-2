<p><p>Ground Control Safety Valves are designed to provide a reliable barrier in subsurface safety valve systems to prevent the uncontrolled flow of fluids during well operations. These valves are installed at the lower end of the production tubing and are actuated by hydraulic or mechanical means. On the other hand, Subsurface Control Safety Valves are equipped with sensors and control systems to monitor and regulate the flow of fluids, enhancing the safety and efficiency of oil and gas production operations.</p></p>
