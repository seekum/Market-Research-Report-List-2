<p><p>A single-phase electric power battery storage inverter is designed to convert direct current (DC) electricity from a battery into alternating current (AC) electricity for use in single-phase electrical systems. These inverters are typically used in residential and small commercial applications. On the other hand, three-phase electric power battery storage inverters are used for larger commercial and industrial applications where three-phase electrical systems are used. These inverters convert DC electricity from a battery into three-phase AC electricity for powering equipment and machinery.</p></p>
